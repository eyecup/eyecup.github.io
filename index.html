What symptoms are caused by electronic addiction

Electronic addiction can cause lots of symptoms, here are few for example.

Feelings of Guilt

Lots of times Electronic Adiction can lead to fellings of guiltyness from past failures

Dishonesty

Electronic use can also lead to minor dishonesty about electronic usage

Extreme Emotions

Useing electronics to much can lead to extreme emotion while using electronics, such as extreme anger or happieness

Physical Symptoms

There are many Physical Symptoms that come with electronic addiction, this can include sleep loss and/or gain and/or loss of friends and/or family.

Loss of Sence of Time

Electronic Addiction can also lead to losing the ability to affectively tell time mentally, meaning that "5 min" can turn to 10 min, ect.

Boredom

Electronic Addiction can lead to boredom or feelings of being lonley when away from electronic devices.

Electronic addiction is getting out of hand.

While electronics are overall good for us, overuse can lead to many mental and/or physical problems down the line such as bad eye sight or other health problems. Electronic overuse can also lead to lazyness and dissatasfaction along with other activities.
