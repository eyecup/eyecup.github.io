Some examples of electronic addiction effects

Feelings of Guilt

Electronic Addiction can lead to feelings of guilt from past failures

Dishonesty

Electronic overuse can lead to minor dishonesty about electronic usage such as how long you've been on it

Extreme Emotions

Using electronics too much can lead to extreme emotions while using electronics, such as extreme anger

Physical Effects

Many Physical Effects that come with electronic addiction such as sleep loss, headaches, blurred vision, etc..

Loss of Sense of Time

Electronic Addiction can also lead to losing the ability to effectively tell time mentally, meaning that what you think is only five minutes can turn into 10 minutes, or 15 minutes to 30 minutes, etc.

Boredom

Electronic Addiction can lead to boredom whenever you don't have an electronic device with you

Electronic addiction is getting out of hand.

While electronics are overall good for us, overuse can lead to many mental and/or physical problems down the line such as bad eyesight or other health problems. Electronic overuse can also lead to laziness and dissatisfaction in doing other activities.
