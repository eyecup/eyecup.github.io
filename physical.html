Physical/Mental effects of Electronic Addiction

What Are The Physical Effects?

Physical symptoms of Internet Addiction: 1.Isolation
2.Children addicted to the internet will show signs of increased aggressiveness and irritability as well as loss of sleep
3.Backache
4.Headaches
5.Weight gain or loss
6.Disturbances in sleep
7.Carpal tunnel syndrome
8.Blurred or strained vision

What Are The Mental Effects?

Some mental symptoms of Internet Addiction
1.Feelings of guilt
2.Dishonesty
3.Euphoric feelings when in front of the computer, as in extreme feelings
4.Unable to keep schedules
5.No sense of time
6.Anxiety
