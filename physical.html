Physical/Mental effects of Electronic Addiction

What Are The Physical Effects?

Physical symptoms of Internet Addiction that children may show signs of are increased aggressiveness and irritability, as well as loss of sleep, backache, headaches, Weight gain or loss, disturbances in sleep, Carpal tunnel syndrome, and blurred or strained vision.
