Physical Effects Of Electronic Addiction

Physical effects of electronic addiction can include backaches from sitting for long periods of time, or headaches and eye strain from watching an electronic for too long. It can also cause weight gain from carelessness, and sleep problems. If you look at a screen for over 2 hours, it can damage your retinal cells and lead to long term vision problems.

What Are The Mental Effects?

Some mental Effects of Internet Addiction Some mental effects of electronic addiction can include dishonesty, extreme emotions, and loss of sense of time.
