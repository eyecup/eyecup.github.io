How can you prevent/treat addiction?

We can prevent addiction in many different ways. The first step is to find out if your addicted to electronics. Alot of people are addicted to electronics but dont realize it. If electronics (outside of required work) use alot of your time and are extremely important, then theres a chance you're addicted.

How can you prevent addiction?

  • Limit yourself to 2 hours of screen time
  • Never procrastinate over doing important activities
  • Know the dangers of using electronics too much
  • Do more activities that involve nature/non electronic devices
  • Allow your boredom sometimes instead of going onto an electronic device/phone
